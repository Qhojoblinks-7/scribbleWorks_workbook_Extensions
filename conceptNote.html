BECE Likely Questions

This page provides a list of likely questions for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) to aid in preparation.

Likely Questions

  1. What are the primary factors affecting student performance?
  2. Explain the role of technology in modern education.
  3. Discuss the importance of continuous assessment.
  4. What strategies can be employed to enhance reading skills?
  5. How can teachers foster a positive learning environment?
  6. Describe the impact of parental involvement on student success.
  7. What are the challenges faced in the current education system?
  8. Illustrate the significance of extracurricular activities in student development.
