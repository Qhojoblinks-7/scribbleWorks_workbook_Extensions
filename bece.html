BECE Related Questions

This section provides a collection of questions that may appear in the BECE examination.

Mathematics Questions

  • 1. If the perimeter of a rectangle is 50 cm and its length is 15 cm, what is its width?
  • 2. A bag contains 3 red balls and 2 blue balls. If a ball is drawn at random, what is the probability that it is red?
  • 3. Calculate the area of a triangle with a base of 10 cm and a height of 5 cm.

Science Questions

  • 1. What is the process by which plants make their food?
  • 2. Define the term "ecosystem."
  • 3. List three forms of energy and provide an example of each.

English Language Questions

  • 1. Write a short essay on the importance of education.
  • 2. Identify the subject and predicate in the sentence: "The cat sat on the mat."
  • 3. Provide synonyms for the following words: happy, sad, fast.
